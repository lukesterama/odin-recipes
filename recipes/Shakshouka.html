Shakshouka

Description

I would like to introduce you to one of my favorite breakfast dishes, Shakshouka! I say breakfast but truthfully Shakshouka can be eaten anytime of the day.

Shakshouka or shakshuka is a delicious combination of eggs poached in a sauce of tomatoes, olive oil, peppers, onions and garlic, and commonly spiced with cumin, paprika and cayenne pepper.

The word Shakshouka loosely translates to all mixed up in Arabic, which is an apt description and hints at the potent spice and exotic flavors of this remarkably simple yet delicious dish.
